About Us

You’re in the business of making people happy.You bring the celebration; I bring the communication. As a restaurant operator, you have to wear different types of hats, even if the responsibilities you take on are not within your expertise. Writing is one of those tasks. It can be intimidating to write something customers find interesting.However, the about page on our site is particularly important for online visitors. Customers are naturally curious, and not just about your menu. An about page tells the story of a business, highlighting what makes a restaurant unique. Everyone loves a good origin story!

Our head builds free brand-based online ordering websites for our restaurant partners,including an About Us section. Our associates, like senior graphic designer, have experience in helping our clients highlights interesting and informative content to include on their websites.

Short Story About Us:

The short story is that Manasa gave up her full-time media job to travel the world. 40 countries, 123 cities and the rest of the world to go. She doesn’t believe in taking adventures alone and that’s why she set up "Crunchy Snow Flake" .To get people everywhere to discover their love for travel & indulgence. Before this,Manasa was working as a Business Reporter & Television Anchor with ET NOW, Bloomberg TV and CNBC TV18.
